Hi there, my name's Ahad Rauf.
I'm an undergraduate junior in EECS at UC Berkeley focused primarily on micrometer-scale robots, commonly known as microrobots. While I've worked for many years on robotics projects of all scales, from developing a computer vision system for autonomous vehicles to building a prosthetic robotic arm to designing a 2 cm drone, I especially enjoy microrobots for the unique challenges in fabricating and controlling structures at such small scales. I get super excited seeing beams thinner than a human hair learn to walk on water, jump, and even fly, and I look forward to helping push this development further through my research and studies.
