Divergence and Curl Properties

$\vec{a} \cdot (\vec{b} \times \vec{c}) = \vec{b} \cdot (\vec{c} \times \vec{a}) = \vec{c} \cdot (\vec{a} \times \vec{b})$
$\vec{a} \times (\vec{b} \times \vec{c}) = \vec{b}(\vec{a} \cdot \vec{c}) - \vec{c}(\vec{a} \cdot \vec{b})$
 $\nabla \times (f\vec{v}) = f(\nabla \times \vec{v}) + (\nabla f) \times \vec{v}$
$\nabla (\vec{v} \cdot \vec{w}) = \vec{v} \times (\nabla \times \vec{w}) + \vec{w} \times (\nabla \times \vec{v}) + (\nabla \cdot \vec{v})\vec{w} + (\nabla \cdot \vec{w})\vec{v}$
$\nabla \cdot (\vec{v} \times \vec{w}) = \vec{w} \cdot (\nabla \times \vec{v}) - \vec{v} \cdot (\nabla \times \vec{w})$
$\nabla (\nabla \times \vec{v}) = 0$
$\nabla \times (\nabla f) = 0$
$\nabla^2F := \nabla \cdot (\nabla F)$       - Definition
$\nabla \times (\nabla \times \vec{v}) = \nabla (\nabla \cdot \vec{v}) - \nabla^2\vec{v})$
$\nabla^2 \vec{v} = \langle \nabla^2 v_x, \nabla^2 v_y, \nabla^2 v_z \rangle$     - Definition
$\oint (\nabla F) \cdot \vec{dr} = 0$
$\oint \vec{v} \cdot \vec{dS} = \int (\nabla \cdot \vec{v}) d^3r$
$\oint \vec{v} \cdot \vec{dr} = \int (\nabla \times \vec{v}) \cdot \vec{dS}$
